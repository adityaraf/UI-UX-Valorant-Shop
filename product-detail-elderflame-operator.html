Elderflame Operator

The Elderflame Operator is part of Valorant's first Ultra Edition skin line, featuring weapons transformed into living, breathing dragons. This skin completely reimagines the Operator, turning it into a dragon that moves, breathes, and roars with each action.

The Elderflame collection represents the pinnacle of skin design in Valorant, with unprecedented levels of animation and detail that make these weapons truly come alive in your hands.

Features:

  • Custom dragon model that breathes and moves as you play
  • Intricate scales and details that shift with the weapon's movement
  • Dragon roar sound effects when firing
  • Unique reload animation where the dragon consumes and regurgitates ammunition
  • Fire-breathing finisher effect that incinerates enemies
  • Custom inspect animation showing the dragon coming to life
  • Available in multiple color variants to match your style
