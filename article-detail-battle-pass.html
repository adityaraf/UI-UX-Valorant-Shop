Valorant Episode 7 Act 3 Battle Pass: All Skins and Rewards

Alex Johnson Senior Valorant Analyst

The new Valorant Episode 7 Act 3 Battle Pass has finally arrived, bringing with it three new skin lines: Imperium, Rune Stone, and Goldwing. In this comprehensive breakdown, we'll explore all the tiers, rewards, and help you decide whether it's worth your VP.

Battle Pass Overview

Priced at 1,000 VP (approximately $10 USD), the Episode 7 Act 3 Battle Pass includes 50 tiers of content, featuring:

  • 9 weapon skins (3 from each of the new skin lines)
  • 3 gun buddies
  • 3 player cards
  • 3 sprays
  • 15 player titles
  • Radianite Points (a total of 130 RP)
  • Exclusive melee weapon (Goldwing Kunai)

The Battle Pass will be available until the end of Episode 7 Act 3, giving players approximately two months to complete all 50 tiers.

Imperium Skin Line

The Imperium collection features a regal, gold-accented design with intricate detailing that gives off a luxury vibe. The weapons included in this skin line are:

Imperium Vandal (Tier 25)

The highlight of the Imperium collection, the Vandal features a sleek gold and black design with royal purple accents. The reload animation includes a subtle golden glow effect that adds to its premium feel.

Imperium Sheriff (Tier 15)

The Sheriff continues the royal theme with gold filigree work along the barrel and a distinctive purple chamber that glows when firing.

Imperium Ares (Tier 35)

The Ares rounds out the collection with the same gold and purple aesthetic, featuring an ornate design along the body of the weapon.

Rune Stone Skin Line

The Rune Stone collection draws inspiration from ancient Nordic runes, featuring stone-like textures with glowing magical inscriptions. The weapons in this line include:

Rune Stone Phantom (Tier 20)

The Phantom features a stone-gray base with glowing blue runes that pulse subtly during gameplay. The firing sound has a slight magical echo to it.

Rune Stone Guardian (Tier 10)

The Guardian features similar runic patterns with a more pronounced glow effect when aiming down sights.

Rune Stone Shorty (Tier 30)

The compact Shorty is covered in dense runic inscriptions that glow brightly when firing.

Goldwing Skin Line

The Goldwing collection features an angelic theme with white, gold, and light blue accents, giving it an ethereal appearance. The weapons in this line include:

Goldwing Operator (Tier 40)

The Operator features elegant wing motifs along the barrel and a golden scope. The firing effect includes a subtle feather particle effect.

Goldwing Spectre (Tier 5)

The Spectre has a sleek white body with gold accents and small wing designs on the magazine.

Goldwing Kunai (Tier 50)

The exclusive melee weapon of this Battle Pass, the Kunai features a white handle with gold blade edges and a subtle blue glow. It includes a unique equip animation where the blade briefly sprouts ethereal wings.

Free Track Rewards

For players who don't purchase the premium Battle Pass, the free track still offers some valuable rewards:

  • Rune Stone Shorty (Tier 30)
  • 2 gun buddies
  • 1 player card
  • 5 player titles
  • 20 Radianite Points

Is It Worth Your VP?

At 1,000 VP for 9 weapon skins (including a melee weapon), the Battle Pass offers significant value compared to purchasing individual skins from the store. The Radianite Points alone are worth approximately 2,000 VP if purchased directly.

The standout items in this Battle Pass are undoubtedly the Imperium Vandal and the Goldwing Kunai. Both feature premium-quality designs that rival some of the more expensive skin collections in the game.

Value Breakdown:

  • 9 weapon skins (estimated value: 900-1,800 VP each)
  • 130 Radianite Points (estimated value: 2,600 VP)
  • Additional cosmetics (gun buddies, cards, sprays, titles)
  • Total estimated value: 10,000+ VP

For players who regularly play Valorant and can complete most of the tiers, the Episode 7 Act 3 Battle Pass represents excellent value. Even if you're only interested in a few of the skins, the included Radianite Points make it a worthwhile purchase.

Tips for Completing the Battle Pass

To ensure you unlock all 50 tiers before the Act ends:

  • Focus on completing your daily and weekly missions
  • Play at least 2-3 matches daily
  • Weekly missions provide the most XP, so prioritize these
  • Spike Rush is the most efficient game mode for quick XP gains
  • Consider purchasing the Battle Pass early to maximize the value from weekly missions

With consistent play and mission completion, most players should be able to reach tier 50 with about 3-4 weeks to spare.

Conclusion

The Episode 7 Act 3 Battle Pass offers an impressive collection of skins and rewards that provide excellent value for its 1,000 VP price tag. The Imperium, Rune Stone, and Goldwing collections each bring a unique aesthetic to the table, ensuring there's something for every player's taste.

Whether you're a casual player looking to expand your collection or a dedicated Valorant enthusiast who wants to maximize value, this Battle Pass is definitely worth considering. The Imperium Vandal and Goldwing Kunai alone justify the purchase for many players.
