Singularity Spectre

The Singularity Spectre is part of the cosmic-themed Singularity collection, featuring weapons that harness the power of black holes and gravitational anomalies. This premium skin transforms the Spectre into a weapon that seems to bend space and time with its otherworldly design and effects.

The Singularity collection is known for its unique animations where parts of the weapon float and orbit around a central gravitational point, creating one of the most visually distinctive skin lines in the game.

Features:

  • Custom model with floating parts that orbit around a central black hole
  • Purple and black color scheme with cosmic energy effects
  • Unique firing sound with space-time distortion effects
  • Special reload animation where the magazine is pulled into a miniature black hole
  • Custom finisher effect that creates a singularity, pulling enemies into another dimension
  • Available in multiple color variants to match your style
